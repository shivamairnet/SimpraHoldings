About Us

WHO WE ARE

Creating Fortunes with Purpose: Transforming the World for a Better Tomorrow

Simpra Holdings Limited is an investment firm dedicated to creating fortunes while improving the world. With over 75 years of collective experience, our team provides not just capital but also valuable insights to help our partners achieve success beyond traditional capital infusion. We focus on small to medium-cap investments in ventures with proven concepts, particularly those creating niche markets or expanding traditional ones.

Founded by industry veterans, Simpra Holdings is a RAK ICC (Offshore) Company with a global investment footprint. Our current portfolio includes companies in sectors such as Infotech, IoT, Renewable Energy, F&B, Security, and Construction.
